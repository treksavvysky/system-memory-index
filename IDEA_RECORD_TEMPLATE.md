Idea Record Template

Purpose

This template defines the standard structure used to capture new ideas in the System Memory Index.

The template ensures ideas are recorded consistently while preserving the original insight that sparked the idea.

Each Idea Record represents a node in the intellectual graph of the ecosystem.

⸻

Template

---
uid: SMI-YYYYMMDD-XXX
type: IDEA
title: <Idea Title>
status: captured
created: YYYY-MM-DD

author: George

parent_uid: null

related_uids: []

doctrine_uid: null
---

# <Idea Title>

## Raw Idea Capture

Record the original thought as it first appeared.  
This section should preserve the idea exactly as it emerged, without editing or refinement.

The purpose of this section is to capture the initial spark before interpretation or analysis alters it.

---

## Clarified Concept

Explain the idea in clearer terms after initial reflection.

Describe what the idea represents and why it might matter.

---

## Context of Discovery

Describe the circumstances under which the idea appeared.

Examples may include:

- conversation
- planning session
- coding experiment
- domain discovery
- system design discussion

Context helps future readers understand the origin and motivation of the idea.

---

## Potential Applications

Describe how the idea might be used.

Possible directions include:

- new systems
- product opportunities
- research exploration
- architectural improvements

---

## Observations

Additional thoughts or insights that emerge after the idea was recorded.

This section can grow over time.

---

## Review Notes

Notes from later evaluation, strategy sessions, or General's Tent discussions.

This section documents how the idea evolved or whether it was promoted into a project or other artifact.


⸻

Template Usage

When a new idea emerges, a new file should be created using this template.

The UID should be generated using the identity scheme defined in the architecture.

The Raw Idea Capture section should be filled immediately to preserve the original thought before it fades.

Additional sections can be expanded later as the idea develops.

⸻

Role in the System

Idea Records represent the entry point of intellectual artifacts into the System Memory Index.

They allow ideas to evolve gradually from raw insight into clarified concepts, structured initiatives, or full projects.

Over time, the network of Idea Records becomes a map of the ecosystem’s conceptual evolution.
